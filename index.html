The Film

Built With Purpose.
Born From Vision.

Mind of Their Own is an independent short film that dares to ask the question every creator eventually confronts: what does it mean to be made for a reason?

Set against otherworldly landscapes and populated by beings that exist on the boundary between machine and soul, the film explores identity, autonomy, and the haunting drive to fulfill one's purpose — even when no one is watching.

"Purpose Without People. A Few Screws Loose. Made For A Reason."
— Three stories. One universe.

Spanning three distinct visual chapters — each drenched in its own palette of amber, teal, and magenta — Mind of Their Own is a hand-crafted work of independent animation filmmaking in the truest sense.
