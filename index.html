The Film

Built With Purpose.
Born From Vision.

Mind of Their Own is an independent short film about a colony of robots that rises from the ashes of a crumbled city in response to a far away radio signal. A team of five robots are sent on a mission to find and save the last remaining humans.

Set against otherworldly landscapes and populated by beings that exist on the boundary between machine and soul, the film explores identity, autonomy, and the haunting drive to fulfill one's purpose — even when no one is watching.

"Purpose Without People. A Few Screws Loose. Made For A Reason."

Drenched in its own distinct visual palette — amber, teal, and magenta — Mind of Their Own is a hand-crafted work of independent filmmaking in the truest sense.
